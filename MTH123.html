MTH123

Computer Mathematics


As expected in a course focused on computers and the sciences behind them, mathematics are
a core element to thinking like a computer scientist. From basic concepts such as algebra, to more advanced
uses of binary, octal, and hexadecimal number systems. These key exercises and knowledge of
computer structure are crucial for any programmer or computer scientist to understand how a computer
works on a fundamental level.

For more information, you can check out the Sault College information page about the course!
