CSD112

Introduction to Web Development


One of the primary uses of computers and nearly any device that connects to the internet is accessing
web pages! In this course, we learn where they started, where they come from and what they are capable of today!
We learn about HTML and CSS, and how they are utilized to make the amazing websites and layouts we're so familiar with!
On top of that, we look into the understanding of internet protocols, and what makes our websites exchange
information under secure connections!

For more information, you can check out the Sault College information page about the course!
